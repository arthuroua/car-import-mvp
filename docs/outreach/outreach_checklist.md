# Outreach Checklist (Copart + IAA)

## Before sending

- Register legal entity and ensure company domain email is active.
- Prepare one-page product description.
- Estimate monthly VIN lookup volume.
- Define required countries and usage rights.

## Must-have legal questions

- Can we publicly display auction images?
- Can we show sold price and lot history in consumer reports?
- What retention period is allowed for cached data?
- Are we allowed to mix Copart and IAA data in one report?
- What attribution text is mandatory?

## Technical onboarding questions

- Full load + incremental update model?
- Rate limits and retry policy?
- Data freshness SLA?
- Change events available (status/estimate updates)?
- Sandbox/test credentials available?

## Decision gates

- Access approved by both sources.
- Signed license terms reviewed by legal counsel.
- Allowed display and retention model implemented in product.
